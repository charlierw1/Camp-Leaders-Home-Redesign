Basic Information

We ask for a profile photo to help our team, interviewers, and your future camp recognise you more easily.

Photo Tips

Please follow the instructions below carefully. If the uploaded photo does not match the requirements, it will be rejected and may delay your application.

  • ✅Your face is centred in the middle of the frame
  • ✅You are in a brightly lit area with a clear background
  • ✅You are the only person visible in the image
  • ✅Your face is clearly visible and not covered (other than medical/religious purposes)
  • ✅A natural and positive smile
  • ❌Social media filters like dog ears or cat faces... 🙀
  • ❌Inappropriate content, such as nudity or offensive content
