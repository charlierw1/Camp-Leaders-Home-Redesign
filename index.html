Welcome back, Jordan!

Next Action: Complete profile

You’re now "Ready to Hire"

This means Camp Directors can view your profile and reach out to arrange a chat and offer you a place at summer camp.

What happens next:

You may hear from a camp straight away or it may take a short while.

Your application will go 'On Review' with camps. Please be aware that the review period is just a few days so follow up with them quickly and select the earliest interview available.

When successful you will receive a "Placement Offer". An offer has an expiry date so please accept or decline promptly.

Important things to do:

Check your emails and Camp Leaders messages regularly for contact from camps.

Go to 'Camps' in the menu to explore our camps and click 'I'm interested' to let us know your favourites.

Sign up for online hiring events to request interviews with camps.

Keep your application moving forward by working through your checklist.

Your placement status explained:

Visible - Camp Directors can view your application. You'll still be able to edit and add more to it, there's always room to tweak something.

On review - A camp is interested in your application. You can request an interview or send them a message. The camp may also reach out by messaging via your profile or by email.

Confirmed - Congratulations! You have accepted a placement offer from a camp. Camp Leaders will now finalise your placement information.

Placed - Your placement information is finalised. Your arrival dates to the US are set. You are now ready to begin your visa process and book your flights.
